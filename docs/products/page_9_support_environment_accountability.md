## Section 3: Design Your 30-Day Container

### Page 9: Support, Environment, and Accountability

This experiment does not happen in a vacuum.

Your environment, relationships, and daily rhythms will either support this work or create friction. Neither is wrong. Both are information.

This page asks you to design your container intentionally. Not perfectly. Just with enough awareness to notice what helps and what makes it harder.

## Places & Tech: Shifting Your Environment

Small changes in your environment can reduce decision fatigue and create friction where you need it.

**Physical environment:**

☐ Remove credit cards from wallet (keep one for true emergencies elsewhere)  
☐ Delete saved payment info from frequently used sites  
☐ Unsubscribe from marketing emails  
☐ Remove shopping apps from phone home screen  
☐ Put cash in envelope system for essentials  
☐ Create a visible reminder of my rules (post-it, phone wallpaper, etc.)
