# Weekly Review Templates

**Use these at the end of each week (Days 7, 14, 21, 28) for structured reflection**

---

## How to Use These Reviews

Each week has a short review template. The questions stay mostly the same, but the focus shifts slightly based on where you are in the challenge.

Set aside 10-15 minutes at the end of each week. Answer the questions honestly. Do not perform. Do not grade yourself. Just notice.

These reviews help you:
- See patterns over time instead of reacting to individual days
- Adjust your C3 strategies based on what you are learning
- Celebrate small wins
- Identify what needs to change

## How to Use Weekly Reviews in Future Rounds

If you run another 30-day challenge later, these templates still work. Just print new copies or copy them into a fresh document.

The questions are designed to stay relevant across multiple cycles. The patterns you notice, the C3 strategies you test, and the adjustments you make will be different each time.

That is the point. This is iterative work, not one-and-done.

Each cycle teaches you something new. Each review captures what you learned so you do not lose it.

---

**Keep these reviews. They are part of your data. Future-you will want to see what this round looked like.**
