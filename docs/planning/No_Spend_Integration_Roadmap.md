## SECTION 4: Voice & Brand Compliance

### Voice Violations to Watch For

From Extraction 10 (lines 86-110):

**DON'T USE** (common mistakes):

| Violation | Example | Fix | Where to Check |
|-----------|---------|-----|----------------|
| Binary framing | "It's not about restriction, it's about freedom" | Remove "It's not X, it's Y" structure | All sections, especially Section 1 |
| Em dashes | "You can pause—or cancel—any subscription" | Use periods or commas instead | All written content |
| Conjunctions starting sentences | "But you also don't have to live there" | "You also don't have to live there" | Daily prompts especially |
| Toxic positivity | "You've got this! Just believe!" | Remove cheerleading, stay grounded | Section 1, daily prompts |
| Willpower framing | "Just say no to impulse buys" | Use pattern recognition language instead | All sections |
| Therapy-speak | "Let's unpack your money trauma" | Stay in pattern observation lane | Section 2, daily prompts |

### Voice Compliance Examples

**GOOD** (from Extraction 10:287):
> "If you spent when you planned not to, that happened. Name it. Own it. You don't have to pretend it didn't occur or spin it into something it wasn't. You also don't have to live there. A slip is a data point, not a verdict. Look at what happened. Figure out what it showed you. Then keep going."

**BAD** (created example):
> "If you broke your commitment, don't beat yourself up! It's not failure, it's just a learning opportunity! Get back on track and you've got this!"

**Why Bad**:
- Toxic positivity ("don't beat yourself up!")
- Binary framing ("It's not X, it's Y")
- Cheerleading ("you've got this!")

### Recommended Voice Review Process

1. **Write all content first** (don't edit while writing)
2. **Run voice checklist** on every page:
   - [ ] No binary framing?
   - [ ] No em dashes?
   - [ ] No sentences starting with conjunctions (in written work)?
   - [ ] No toxic positivity?
   - [ ] Conversational and direct?
   - [ ] Teaching WITH, not AT?
3. **Compare to examples** in Extraction 10 (lines 260-305)
4. **Get external review** using voice guide as rubric

## SECTION 10: Critical Success Factors

### What Will Make or Break This Product

**SUCCESS FACTORS**:

1. ✅ **Framework Alignment** - Using Aligned Life (not Chasing Series) as foundation
2. ✅ **Voice Consistency** - Shame-free, conversational, anti-perfectionism throughout
3. ✅ **Daily Prompts Quality** - These ARE the product; must be excellent
4. ✅ **Flexibility** - Multiple participation formats, adaptable rules
5. ✅ **Pattern Positioning** - Optional tools, not mandatory framework

**RISK FACTORS**:

1. ❌ **Forcing Chasing patterns** onto daily structure (framework violation)
2. ❌ **Voice violations** (binary framing, toxic positivity, instructional tone)
3. ❌ **Giving away premium content** for free (repeat of PLR mistake)
4. ❌ **Overwhelming users** with too much required tracking
5. ❌ **Incomplete daily prompts** (rushing to launch without finishing core content)
