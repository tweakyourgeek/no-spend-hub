## C3 Framework (Cut, Cancel, Combine)

### Definitions:
- **CUT**: Reduce frequency or eliminate partially (daily coffee -> twice a week)
- **CANCEL**: Stop paying entirely OR downgrade to lower tier
- **COMBINE**: Consolidate services, share costs, or find cheaper alternatives
