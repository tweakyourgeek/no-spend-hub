## Risks and Mitigation

### Risk 1: Guide not finished by January
**Mitigation**: Push launch to February, use Q1 challenge as soft launch with existing materials

### Risk 2: Technical issues with Skool
**Mitigation**: Beta test in December, have backup plan (Facebook Group temporarily)

### Risk 3: Bundle traffic doesn't convert
**Mitigation**: Have strong email sequences, don't rely solely on bundles

### Risk 4: Founding 100 doesn't fill
**Mitigation**: Extend founding member offer, increase urgency messaging

### Risk 5: Retention is low
**Mitigation**: Increase community engagement, add more value, survey members
