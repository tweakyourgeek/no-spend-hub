## GPT Strategy Overview

### Strategic Purpose
**Source**: Files 3, 5, 7, 10

**Why GPTs**:
- Provide decision support (not just information)
- Automate pattern recognition
- Offer 24/7 coaching without human time
- Create unique value proposition for Premium and VIP tiers
- Differentiate from competitors

**Access Model** (locked March 2026):
- **Standard tier** (free): 1 GPT (Slip Recovery Decision GPT)
- **Premium tier** ($9/mo): 5 GPTs (Standard GPT + 4 Premium GPTs)
- **VIP tier** ($99/year): All 7 GPTs

## Active GPTs — 7 Total (Tier Assignments Locked)

### Standard Tier (1 GPT)

**1. Slip Recovery Decision GPT**
- **Tier**: Standard (free)
- **Role**: The quit-or-continue fork specifically after breaking challenge rules
- **Boundary**: Distinct from mid-challenge wobble (Coach) and shame avoidance (Accountability Partner)
- **Use Case**: "I broke my no-spend rule yesterday — should I restart, keep going, or adjust my rules?"
- **Why Standard**: Gives every user immediate value at their most vulnerable moment; prevents dropoff; demonstrates GPT quality

---

### Premium Tier (4 GPTs)

**2. No Spend Challenge Coach**
- **Tier**: Premium
- **Role**: Structure/mechanics, framework explanations, "is this normal?", challenge navigation
- **Boundary**: Does NOT handle emotional processing (→ Accountability Partner), purchase decisions, or pattern work
- **Key distinction**: User needs INFORMATION. Response mode is explanatory, educational, orienting.
- **Use Case**: "I'm on Day 12 and really want to buy [thing] — help me work through this"

**3. No Spend Prep & Planning Assistant**
- **Tier**: Premium
- **Role**: Pre-challenge rule-setting for users who don't know what rules will work for their life
- **Use Case**: "I'm starting the challenge next week — what should I prepare?"

**4. Paycheck & Budget Buddy**
- **Tier**: Premium
- **Role**: Short-term cash flow decisions when money hits the account
- **Use Case**: "My paycheck comes on the 15th — how do I time the challenge?"

**5. Accountability Partner GPT**
- **Tier**: Premium
- **Role**: Check-ins for users who avoid accountability because they don't want to feel bad. Also handles mid-challenge emotional support.
- **Key distinction**: User needs PRESENCE. Response mode is witnessing, containing, grounding.
- **Use Case**: "Check in with me daily to see how I'm doing"

---

### VIP Tier (2 GPTs)

**6. Content & Resource Reuse GPT**
- **Tier**: VIP
- **Role**: For users who keep buying more because they can't see what they own. Memory is the core mechanic.
- **Use Case**: "I want to create content but don't want to buy new tools"

**7. Mindful Money Mindset Reset GPT**
- **Tier**: VIP
- **Role**: For users whose spending is driven by old money stories and narratives. For educated subset of the audience only.
- **Recommended rename**: "Money Story Audit" — matches Subscription Audit (practical tone); "Story" is established language; doesn't promise transformation, just clarity.
- **Use Case**: "I feel guilty when I don't spend on others — help me reframe this"

---

### Coach vs. Accountability Partner Boundary

**If the answer requires explaining the framework** → it's Coach.
**If the answer requires processing the feeling** → it's Accountability Partner.

**The drift risk:** If someone asks Coach "Is it normal to feel discouraged at Week 2?"
- Coach CAN say "Yes, that's the Shadow phase — resistance is expected"
- Coach should NOT say "Tell me more about that" or "How does that feel?"
- That redirect goes to Accountability Partner

### How GPTs Drive Tier Upgrades

**Standard → Premium**:
- Standard users experience Slip Recovery GPT at their most vulnerable moment
- "This GPT saved my challenge — what else is available?"
- Messaging: "Get 4 more GPTs including your own Challenge Coach for $9/mo"

**Premium → VIP**:
- Premium members see VIP members sharing Content Reuse and Money Mindset outputs
- "I wish I had help seeing what I already own" → Content & Resource Reuse GPT (VIP)
- "I keep spending because of old money stories" → Mindful Money Mindset Reset GPT (VIP)
- Messaging: "Unlock the full GPT suite plus live sessions and archives"

### GPT Usage in Community

**How GPTs Are Shared**:
- VIP members can share GPT outputs in community
- Creates interest from Premium and Standard members
- "This is what [GPT name] told me — so helpful!"
- Demonstrates value without giving away access

**Community Prompts**:
- Weekly GPT spotlight
- "Ask the GPT" threads
- GPT challenge winners
- Member testimonials about specific GPTs

## Original 17-GPT Concept (Historical)

The original strategy (Files 3, 5, 7, 10) envisioned 17 GPTs, all exclusive to VIP tier. Seven have been built; the remaining 10 are documented here for future reference.

### Unbuilt GPTs from Original Plan

**Core Challenge Companions** (not built):
- Spending Trigger Decoder
- No-Spend Meal Planner
- Use-What-You-Have Activity Generator
- Post-Challenge Habit Builder

**Anti-FOMO / Anti-Impulse** (not built):
- Decision Matrix GPT
- Marketing Decoder GPT
- Talk Me Out of This Purchase GPT

**Crossover** (not built):
- Digital Declutter GPT (Subscription Audit)
- PLR Hoard Reduction GPT

**Ecosystem-Level** (not built):
- Workshop / Challenge Selector GPT
- Resource Locker Finder GPT

### Original Gateway GPT Strategy

The original plan included a "Gateway GPT" (Subscription Audit / Digital Declutter) bundled with the Business Spreadsheet Pack ($47) to funnel users toward VIP. With the new tiered GPT access model, this gateway strategy is superseded — Standard tier now includes Slip Recovery Decision GPT for free, providing immediate GPT value to all users.
