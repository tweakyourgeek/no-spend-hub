# How to Use This Challenge

## 4 Steps to Navigate Your 30-Day Business No Spend

### 1. Before You Start
Do a light pass on Section 1 (inventory), Section 2 (30-day direction), and Section 3 (rules) in the Workbook. Complete "Before You Begin," Money Feelings Mind Map, and your first word cloud + Word of the Week in the Journal.

### 2. Week 1 – Noticing & Naming
Use the Week 1 daily pages + Week 1 Wrap-Up to notice urges, surprises, and first patterns. Do your first Weekly Check-in in the Workbook; keep numbers approximate if needed.

### 3. Weeks 2-3 – Urges & Patterns
Work through Week 2 (Urges & Alternatives) and Week 3 (Patterns & Pressure Points) daily pages + wrap-ups. Complete Weekly Check-ins; optionally use the Spreadsheet Pack for weekly and before/after views.

### 4. Week 4 & Beyond – Integration & Next Steps
Use Week 4 (Integration & Self-Respect) pages + End-of-Challenge Reflection to decide what you're keeping. Finish Section 5 Debrief (and Section 6 if you have spreadsheets), then note your next gentle experiments.
