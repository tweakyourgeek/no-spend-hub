# Three Pillar Boxes - Tool Overview

## Your Three Tools

### 📘 Workbook
**Purpose:** Structure, tracking, before/after

The Workbook is your operational spine. Use it for:
- Financial inventory
- Setting your 30-day direction
- Creating rules and boundaries
- Weekly check-ins
- End-of-challenge debrief

[Download Workbook #]

---

### 📔 Journal
**Purpose:** Daily noticing, feelings, patterns

The Journal is where you track the internal work. Use it for:
- Daily prompts and reflections
- Emotional patterns
- Spending triggers
- Weekly wrap-ups
- Integration and self-respect work

[Download Journal #]

---

### 📊 Spreadsheet Pack
**Purpose:** Optional. For people who like numbers.

The Spreadsheet Pack is infrastructure, not requirement. Use it if:
- You want visual dashboards
- You like tracking subscriptions in detail
- You want before/after comparison charts
- Numbers help you make decisions

[Download Spreadsheet Pack #]
