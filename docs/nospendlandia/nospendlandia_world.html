The Origin

The Fool arrives with a bindle and a lot of questions. Not carelessness. Just a missing map.

The real enemy in NoSpendLandia has no face and no dramatic entrance. It is the absence of tools. It is the voice that says you should already know this. Hard to read a map you have never been handed. Hard to do something you were never taught.

Nobody gave you a map.
We made you one.

Three quests. Nine characters. One Fool who learns to read their own terrain and returns home knowing what they did not before.
