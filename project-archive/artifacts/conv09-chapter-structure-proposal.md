# New Chapter Structure Proposal (5-Chapter Reorganization)

Context: After determining that the PLR workbook's chapter titles, subheadings, and instructional body text all needed replacing (not just paragraph swaps), user chose Option A: complete reorganization. This is the proposed new structure.

## Why Complete Reorganization:

The original PLR structure was:
1. Chapter organization (5 chapters in same order as PLR)
2. All chapter titles lifted from PLR
3. Approximately 15-20 subheadings from PLR
4. ~10 pages of instructional body text from PLR

Option A (chosen): Reorganize completely - new chapter structure, new subheadings, rewritten content. Takes longer but 100% defensible.

Option B (rejected): Keep 5-chapter flow but rename everything and rearrange some subsections. Faster but closer to PLR bones.

## Integration with User's Frameworks:

The rewrite was planned to incorporate:
- **Chasing Ambrosia** framework (the belief that the next purchase will fix everything) - drives impulse buying, course-hoarding, "invest in yourself" overspending
- **Chasing Rainbows** framework (the myth that more income will fix fear/doubt) - drives scarcity-based spending and difficulty with delayed gratification
- **Money messages -> money stories -> money patterns** progression from Aligned Life workshops
- De-woo'd for the no-spend audience (keep the insight, lose the ritual language)

## Note:

The daily prompts (pages 17-50 of the original) stay exactly as they are since those were already original content. Only the instructional framework sections needed reorganization and rewriting.
