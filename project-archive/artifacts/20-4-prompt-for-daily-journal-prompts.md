## PROMPT FOR DAILY JOURNAL PROMPTS

I'm creating 30 daily journal prompts for a no-spend challenge guide. The challenge is organized into 4 weekly themes, each tied to specific spending patterns from my "Chasing Series" framework.

**Weekly Structure:**

**Week 1: Starting the Experiment (Days 1-7)**
Primary patterns: Rainbows (buying the next fix/solution), Unicorns (chasing ideal you)
Focus: Entry energy, pausing future-fix spending, defining "good enough"

**Week 2: Meeting Your Resistance (Days 8-14)**
Primary patterns: Ambrosia (buying to change feelings), Sunshine (mood-boost spending)
Focus: Emotional and mood-based spending urges, non-spending ways to meet needs

**Week 3: Identity, Visibility, and Enoughness (Days 15-21)**
Primary patterns: Stardust (identity/legitimacy purchases), Unicorns (comparison)
Focus: How identity, legitimacy, and comparison drive "looking the part" purchases

**Week 4: Desire, Ambiguity, and What's Next (Days 22-30)**
Primary patterns: Moonbeams (fantasy/someday purchases), all patterns mixed
Focus: Vague wanting, fantasy purchases, integration, choosing next steps

**Each daily page needs:**
1. **Day title/theme** (3-5 words)
2. **Context paragraph** (3-4 lines) - sets up why this matters today
3. **Main prompt** - the question/reflection they write about
4. **Optional second prompt or action** (not every day needs this)

**Suggested daily themes from my blueprint:**

[Then paste the day-by-day themes from the blueprint]

**Voice guidelines:**
- Conversational and direct
- Acknowledges spending patterns serve protective functions
- Anti-perfectionism, shame-free
- "You can't do this wrong"
- Teaching WITH readers, not AT them
- Avoid: binary framing, em dashes, generic self-help voice, toxic positivity, willpower framing

**For each day (1-30), give me:**
- Day X Theme: [title]
- Context: [3-4 line paragraph]
- Main Prompt: [the reflection question]
- Optional: [second prompt or action if needed]

Start with Week 1 (Days 1-7).
