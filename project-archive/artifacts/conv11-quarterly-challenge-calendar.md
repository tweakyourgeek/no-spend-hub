| **Q4** | Holiday Spending Reset | Nov-Dec | Intentional holidays |

---

## What Free Tier Gets for Each Challenge

- Worksheet PDF (basic tracker, prompts, completion page)
- Daily prompts posted in Skool
- Weekly check-in threads
- Completion badge/shareable graphic

## What They DON'T Get (Differentiator)

- Full pattern framework (that's in the paid journal)
- Detailed trackers and spreadsheets
- Premium community features (VIP tier)
- Live calls

## Conversion Path

Free challenge -> "Want the full experience? Get the journal."

## Key Decision

**30 Bags in 30 Days** was repositioned from paid product ($9-14) to free quarterly community challenge. This was the domino that knocked everything into place. Once 30 Bags became a free community driver, the whole quarterly rhythm emerged.

The journal can reference these challenges: "Check out the Skool community for this and other challenges or micro-challenges that you may want to do during the month."
