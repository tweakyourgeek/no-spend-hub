**Q: Do I need Excel or special software?**
A: No. This runs on Google Sheets, which is free with any Google account. Works on desktop, tablet, or phone.

**Q: Can I customize the categories?**
A: Yes. You can set up to 15 income categories and 24 expense categories based on your actual business spending.

**Q: How often do I need to update it?**
A: Monthly is recommended. The tracker is designed for end-of-month updates, not daily entry (unless your cash flow requires it).

**Q: Will this work for my type of business?**
A: If you're a solopreneur, freelancer, service provider, online business owner, or small business with straightforward income/expenses, yes. If you have complex inventory, payroll, or multiple entities, you may need more robust software.

**Q: What if I don't know accounting terminology?**
A: The tracker uses plain language. Income is money in, expenses are money out. The categories are customizable so you can name them whatever makes sense to you.

**Q: Can I track multiple years?**
A: Each tracker covers one calendar year. You can purchase again for subsequent years or make copies of the template.

**Q: Do I get updates if you improve the template?**
A: This is a one-time purchase of the current version. Any major updates would be new products.

**Q: What if I've never done my own bookkeeping before?**
A: Start by entering last month's numbers. The visual dashboard will show you immediately where you stand. The process gets easier each month.
