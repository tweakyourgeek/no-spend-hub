## 30-Day Daily Prompt Mapping: Lab Loop + Pillars + Lenses

### Week 1: Chasing (The Curiosity Phase)
- Primary Lens: Being (who am I being with money?)
- Questions: What am I reaching for? What does my spending reveal about desire?

### Week 2: Shadow (The Integration Phase)
- Primary Lens: Feeling (what am I avoiding, soothing, or seeking?)
- Questions: What patterns protect me? What do my habits value?

### Week 3: Getting (The Application Phase)
- Primary Lens: Acting (what happens when I change behavior?)
- Questions: What works? What doesn't? What's the data showing?

### Week 4: Reflection (The Assimilation Phase)
- Primary Lens: Showing Up (how does this affect my life/relationships?)
- Questions: What did I learn? Where do I go next?

The **Chasing patterns** show up throughout as recognition tools, but they're not the STRUCTURE. The **Lab Loop** is the structure.
