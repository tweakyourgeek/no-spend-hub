# Expanded Suite Spreadsheet Idea List (10 Additional Workbooks)

From the journal conversation, these expanded suite spreadsheets were identified but **not yet built**:

1. **Numbers Snapshot** - Quick financial snapshot for challenge setup
2. **Essential vs Non-Essential Sort** - Categorize spending into buckets
3. **Urge & Spend Log** - Track spending urges and actual purchases
4. **30-Day Dashboard** - Overview metrics for the full challenge
5. **Subscription Audit Tool** - Deep dive into recurring charges
6. **"What I Already Own" Inventory** - List tools, resources, subscriptions already owned that could replace new purchases
7. **No-Spend Streak Tracker** - Track consecutive no-spend days
8. **Post-Challenge Comparison Report** - Compare pre and post challenge metrics
9. **Multi-Round Progress Tracker** - For people doing repeat cycles; compares Round 1 vs Round 2 vs Round 3 metrics over time
10. **Quick-Reference Chasing Patterns Cheat Sheet** - Quick reference with all 6 patterns, triggers, and interventions
