# Lead Magnet Naming Options (6 Alternatives for Time Value Calculator)

Context: The Time Value Calculator is PLR, so others may be selling it. These were 6 alternative name suggestions to make the product distinctly branded.

## Top Pick:

**"The Strategic Spending Calculator"**
- Clear what it does
- Positions it as decision-making, not just tracking
- Fits the "pattern recognition over manifestation" vibe
- Not cutesy, very you

## Other Solid Options:

**"The Delegation Decision Calculator"**
- Specific to the use case
- More niche/proprietary sounding
- Might be too narrow though?

**"The Worth-It Calculator"**
- Conversational, direct
- Fits your voice
- A bit more casual

**"The Business Investment Calculator"**
- Professional
- Reframes "spending" as "investing"
- Might sound too corporate?

**"The Time ROI Calculator"**
- Accurate to what it does
- Business-y but clear
- ROI is searchable/familiar

**"The Smart Spend Calculator"**
- Simple, clear
- Not revolutionary but functional
- Easy to remember

## Recommendation:

Go with **"The Strategic Spending Calculator"**

**Why:**
- It's clear and descriptive
- "Strategic" makes it sound premium/thoughtful
- Works perfectly with your no-spend challenge positioning
- Doesn't sound like every other "time value" calculator
- Fits your brand voice (direct, practical, no BS)

**How it sounds in the funnel:**

> "Download The Strategic Spending Calculator and stop guessing which business expenses are actually worth it."

> "Your No-Spend Challenge Workbook helps you recognize patterns. The Strategic Spending Calculator helps you make data-driven decisions about what to keep, what to cut, and what to delegate."

**Note:** The Time Value Calculator was later abandoned as a B2B lead magnet in favor of Simple Business Bookkeeping (Easy Bookkeeping v2 renamed). These naming options are historical reference only.
