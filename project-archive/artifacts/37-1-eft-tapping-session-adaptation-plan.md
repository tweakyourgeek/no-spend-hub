## EFT Tapping Session Adaptation Plan for No Spend Collective

### Concept
Adapt an existing EFT tapping transcript for the No Spend community, reframing "following through" language to spending/money patterns language.

### Recommended Specs
- **Length:** 5-7 minutes (original runs ~10 minutes with a lot of repetition)
- **Intro:** 30-second orientation at the start for EFT newcomers
  - Something like: "We're going to say some things out loud that might feel uncomfortably true. That's the point."

### Language Reframing
- Swap frame to: "my relationship with spending is my data"
- Lean into pattern recognition angle
- "I'm changing the story that spending means I failed"
- Connect to Chasing Patterns (the emotional work the members need around money)

### Content Connections
The original transcript covers emotional work that maps directly to No Spend themes:
- Fear of being seen
- Self-sabotage loop
- Comparing yourself to others who are executing while you're still spinning
- Watching someone else launch what you thought of first

### Production Ideas
- Record one per quarter tied to each seasonal challenge
- Q1 declutter guilt is genuinely different from Q4 holiday spending spiral
- Could become a signature piece of the Skool experience
- Members come back to it every round - where you are in life changes what lands
- Month one hitting differently than month six is a feature worth naming out loud
