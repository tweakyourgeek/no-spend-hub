* Tip 1: Start by entering last month's numbers first. Less overwhelming than real-time tracking, and you'll immediately see patterns.
* Tip 2: Set up expense categories based on where money actually goes, not where you think it should go. Check your bank statements.
* Tip 3: Update monthly (first week of new month). Don't torture yourself with daily tracking unless cash flow requires it.
* Tip 4: Watch the profit trend, not just monthly numbers. One bad month means nothing. Three bad months means change something.
