# Lead Magnet Naming Final Decisions

## Final Names (Decided):

**B2C:** My Cash Envelope System
- People instantly know what it is
- "My" makes it yours/personal
- Still searchable ("cash envelope system" is the search term)
- Honest about what it does
- Not trying to be something it's not

**B2B:** Simple Business Bookkeeping
- Professional, straightforward
- Avoids "Easy" (which is in the PLR title "Easy Bookkeeping v2")
- Still conveys the "not overwhelming" benefit
- Clean, clear, searchable
- No cutesy "My" needed for B2B audience

## How We Got Here:

The naming went through multiple iterations:

1. **Cash Envelope System** was originally considered for rename to avoid PLR overlap. Options explored included:
   - "The Challenge Savings Tracker"
   - "The 30-Day Savings Envelope"
   - "Digital Cash Envelope Tracker"
   - "Digital Savings Envelope"
   - "Visual Savings Dashboard"
   - "Savings Goal Organizer"
   - "My Savings Envelopes"

2. **User realization:** "Cash Envelope System" is what people SEARCH FOR and RECOGNIZE. The PLR creators didn't pull that name out of nowhere. By trying to be too clever, you make it LESS discoverable. Just add "My" to claim it.

3. **Easy Bookkeeping v2** was renamed to avoid using the same PLR title. Options explored included:
   - "The Business Spending Snapshot"
   - "The 30-Day Business Finance Tracker"
   - "My Business Bookkeeping"
   - "Business Bookkeeping Tracker"
   - "Easy Business Bookkeeping"

4. **User decided:** "Simple" over "Easy" to avoid matching PLR title exactly. B2B doesn't need the "My" softening.

## Key Insight:

These are **standalone valuable products** that happen to work well as lead magnets for the challenge. They need to stand on their own merit. The challenge is the context where people discover them, but they're not "challenge products."

- **B2C (My Cash Envelope System):** SKU 0021 from Spreadsheetables vault
- **B2B (Simple Business Bookkeeping):** SKU 0516, renamed from "Easy Bookkeeping v2"
