## 1. Chasing Rainbows

**Primary Phase:** Entry / Reset

**When it shows up:**
- Early excitement and planning energy
- Urges to "set everything up right"
- Buying clarity, systems, or future fixes

**Role in the Challenge:**
- Normalize future-fix thinking
- Interrupt spending that disguises itself as preparation

**Key No-Spend Intervention:**
Pause future-fix thinking. Work with what already exists.

---

## 2. Chasing Unicorns

**Primary Phase:** Entry -> Early Resistance

**When it shows up:**
- Comparison shopping
- Research spirals
- "I'm not buying, I'm just looking"

**Role in the Challenge:**
- Surface decision avoidance that feels productive
- Reframe time and attention as spendable resources

**Key No-Spend Intervention:**
Choose enough, not perfect.

---

## 3. Chasing Ambrosia

**Primary Phase:** Early Resistance

**When it shows up:**
- After long or emotionally heavy days
- Late-night scrolling and treating
- "I deserve something" purchases

**Role in the Challenge:**
- Anchor emotional safety
- Prevent self-punishment and rebound spending

**Key No-Spend Intervention:**
Meet the need directly instead of outsourcing it to money.

---

## 4. Chasing Stardust

**Primary Phase:** Mid-Challenge

**When it shows up:**
- Comparison and visibility moments
- Feeling behind, unpolished, or not legitimate enough
- Identity-driven upgrades

**Role in the Challenge:**
- De-link identity from consumption
- Name legitimacy anxiety without shame

**Key No-Spend Intervention:**
You do not need new tools to be who you already are.

---

## 5. Chasing Sunshine

**Primary Phase:** Any time (commonly mid-challenge)

**When it shows up:**
- Boredom or restlessness
- Wanting a lift rather than a solution
- "This would cheer me up" spending

**Important Timing Note:**
Often appears after emotional effort, not failure.

**Role in the Challenge:**
- Prevent "harmless" spending creep
- Normalize mood-regulation urges

**Key No-Spend Intervention:**
Change the state, not the cart.

---

## 6. Chasing Moonbeams

**Primary Phase:** Late Challenge / Reflection

**When it shows up:**
- Vague wanting
- Fantasy purchases
- Longing without a clear object

**Role in the Challenge:**
- Build tolerance for ambiguity
- Allow desire without acting on it

**Key No-Spend Intervention:**
Let the desire exist without answering it.

---

## Locked Pattern Definitions

| Pattern | Core Belief |
|---------|-------------|
| Ambrosia | "This purchase will fix how I feel." |
| Rainbows | "This resource will finally make it all work." (Pot of gold thinking) |
| Unicorns | "I need this to be the version of myself I'm supposed to be." |
| Stardust | "This will make me feel like myself again." (Partial) |
| Sunshine | "I've earned this / I deserve a treat." (Partial) |
| Moonbeams | Vague longing / fantasy purchases (Not fully documented) |
