## Key Design Differences from Old "Pre-Challenge Budgeting Exercise"

1. **Categories renamed** to match how people actually think ("Roof + Basics," "Mystery Leaks" instead of generic "Rent/Mortgage," "Miscellaneous")
2. **"Essential Y/N" column killed** and replaced with "Notes to yourself"
3. **Savings calculation removed** entirely; replaced with pattern-focused reflection prompts
4. **Pattern Flag section added** connecting each Chasing pattern to specific budget categories to watch
5. **Tone shift** from "calculate your potential savings" homework to "get a loose picture" sketch
