# Bundle Acquisition Strategy Outline (3-Bundle Sequence)

## The Sequence Strategy

**Bundle #1 — Cat Griffin "Kickoff Bundle" (January 2026 - FREE)**
- **Product**: My Cash Envelope System (B2C version)
- **Type**: Google Sheets template
- **Bundle Price**: FREE (no minimum)
- **CTA**: Join FREE Skool tier
- **Goal**: Mass list building and awareness
- **Expected Volume**: High - free bundles typically get thousands of downloads

This is the **volume play** - getting your name and your spreadsheet quality in front of a huge audience with zero barrier to entry. The Cash Envelope System showcases your spreadsheet skills while being useful enough that people actually use it, which then makes them aware of your full ecosystem.

**Bundle #2 — Penny Wise Skills Bundle (February 2026 - $5+)**
- **Product**: Personal Budgeting Spreadsheet (from Spreadsheet Pack)
- **Bundle Price**: $5+ minimum
- **CTA**: Drive to FREE Skool tier with upgrade messaging
- **Goal**: Volume lead generation with a quality filter (the $5 barrier)

Recommendation: Submit a personal budgeting spreadsheet that's substantial enough to justify the $5+ price point but doesn't cannibalize your full Spreadsheet Pack ($7-17). Think of it as a "gateway spreadsheet" that makes them want more.

**Bundle #3 — Master Your Money (March 2026 - $27+)**

- **Goal**: Premium audience ready to invest

## The Strategy

1. **January (Griffin - FREE)**: Mass awareness, show them what you can do
2. **February (Penny Wise - $5+)**: Quality-filtered audience, still accessible
3. **March (Master Your Money - $27+)**: Premium audience ready to invest

Each bundle steps up in price point and filters for increasingly committed buyers, while all three drive traffic back to your Skool community at different tier entry points.
