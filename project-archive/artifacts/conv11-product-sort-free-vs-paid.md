# Complete Product Sort: Free vs. Paid

## JOURNAL (CORE PAID PRODUCT) ~70-75 pages

**Section 1: Before You Begin (3-4 pages)**
- Welcome, How It Works, Money Story Check-In

**Section 2: Chasing Series Lens (3-4 pages)**
- Meet the Patterns, Spot Your Pattern, Pattern-to-Spending Mapping

**Section 3: Design Your Container (7-8 pages)**
- Numbers Snapshot (NEW), Decision Tree, Essentials Brain Dump, Non-Essentials Brain Dump, Rules & Safeguards, Support & Environment, Commitment Page

**Section 4: 30-Day Ritual (38-42 pages)**
- 4 weekly overviews, 30 daily pages (with habit checkboxes + transaction log built in), 4 weekly reflections with habit tracker grid

**Section 5: Integration (6-7 pages)**
- What Changed, Pattern Debrief, Before/After Data, Next Cycle, You Did It page, Resources

**Reference Pages (8-10 pages)**
- Pattern Quick Reference, Mantras, Comfort Menu, Free Activities, Social Scripts, Common Mistakes, FAQ

**Tracking Pages (4 pages)**
- 30-Day Habit Grid, Streak Tracker, "Almost Bought It" Log, Wish List

**Included:** One sticker sheet, printable wall tracker, wallet cards
