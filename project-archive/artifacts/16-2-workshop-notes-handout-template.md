# Workshop Notes Handout Template (During)

**Purpose**: Active listening scaffold for processing IN THE MOMENT

**Structure** — Following the 5 phases with active listening prompts:

---

**Invitation:**
- What did I hear? (paraphrasing)
- What feelings came up? (verbalizing emotions)

**Recognition:**
- What patterns do I recognize? (noting cues)
- Questions I have: (clarifying)

**The Lens:**
- The lens in my own words: (paraphrasing)
- How does this reframe what I thought before? (responding to feelings)

**Lived Reality:**
- Examples I'm noticing: (noting cues)
- What else do I want to understand? (encouraging/clarifying)

**Integration:**
- What's landing for me? (listen for total meaning)
- Next steps/questions: (waiting to disclose — holding space for their own processing)

---

**Dual Purpose:**
- Workshop engagement tool
- Teaches active listening skills through use
