## The Chasing Series - No-Spend Challenge Integration Map (Canonical)

### Design Principle

The Chasing patterns are recognition lenses, not lessons to complete. They activate at different moments when spending urges tend to surface.

---

### Challenge Arc Overview

| Phase of Challenge | What's Happening Internally | Chasing Patterns That Activate |
|--------------------|----------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Entry / Reset | Hope, motivation, desire for change | Rainbows, Unicorns |
| Early Resistance | Discomfort, depletion, emotional friction | Ambrosia |
| Mid-Challenge | Comparison, identity wobble | Stardust |
| Mood Swings | Boredom, restlessness, low-grade dissatisfaction | Sunshine |
| Late Challenge | Fatigue, longing, ambiguity | Moonbeams |
| Ongoing Awareness | Mixed, contextual | All patterns as lenses |

---

### Weekly Mapping (Rough Structure)

| Week | Phase | Patterns to Surface | Focus |
|------|-------|---------------------|-------|
| Pre-challenge / Day 1 | Entry/Reset | Rainbows, Unicorns | "Notice prep-mode spending urges" |
| Days 2-7 | Early Resistance | Ambrosia | "Notice comfort-seeking urges" |
| Days 8-14 | Mid-Challenge | Stardust | "Notice comparison/identity urges" |
| Days 8-21 | Mood Swings | Sunshine | "Notice boredom/mood-lift urges" |
| Days 22-30 | Late Challenge | Moonbeams | "Notice vague longing" |

---

### Key Interventions (One Per Pattern)

- **Rainbows:** "Pause future-fix thinking. Work with what already exists."
- **Unicorns:** "Choose enough, not perfect."
- **Ambrosia:** "Meet the need directly instead of outsourcing it to money."
- **Stardust:** "You do not need new tools to be who you already are."
- **Sunshine:** "Change the state, not the cart."
- **Moonbeams:** "Let the desire exist without answering it."
