**You do not have to write in this journal to use it.**

Some people will write paragraphs. Some will write one sentence. Some will record a voice memo on their phone while driving. Some will talk it out with a friend or post about it on social media.

The prompt is the prompt. How you respond to it is up to you.

If you want to blog your way through this challenge, do that. If you want to record a video each day, do that. If you want to post your reflection on Instagram or in the Skool community, do that.

The daily page gives you a place to land if you want paper. If your reflection happens somewhere else, just come back to the page and fill in the check-in section at the bottom. That's enough. The data matters whether you write here or not.
