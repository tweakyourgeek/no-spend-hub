### Usage Notes
- **No stark white anywhere.** Use cream (#F8F1F2) or off-white (#F9F0F5) for cards and backgrounds.
- **Plum (#493751)** is the darkest brand color. Use for grounding/accent, not charcoal substitutes.
- **Navy (#3B3B58)** is the primary dark color for text, section backgrounds (e.g., Chasing Patterns section), and outlined buttons.
- **Mauve (#B375A0)** is the primary brand/CTA color.
- **Sage (#A6C9BB)** is for success/completion states.
- **Peach (#E8B4A0)** stays in the palette per user decision. Not removed from the site.
- **Periwinkle (#A7AFC8)** has WCAG contrast concerns on light backgrounds. Replace with navy (#3B3B58) for text on light backgrounds.

### Fonts
- **Cormorant Garamond** — Display, headlines, italic callouts (serif)
- **Open Sans** — Body, nav, buttons, UI labels (sans-serif)
- NOT Playfair Display. NOT DM Sans. Those were used in error in early prototypes.
