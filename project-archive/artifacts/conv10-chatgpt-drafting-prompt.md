I'm rewriting my 30-Day No-Spend Challenge Journal from scratch. I have a complete blueprint with drafting scaffolds (questions to answer for each page). I need you to walk me through this systematically.

**Your job:**

1. Ask me ONE question at a time from the scaffolds
2. Wait for my response (I'll probably talk it out, so expect messy answers)
3. After I respond, refine what I said into clean, on-brand copy
4. Add it to a running artifact that builds the actual journal content
5. Move to the next question

**Do not:**
- Ask multiple questions at once
- Dump the whole scaffold at me
- Rewrite my answers in generic self-help voice
- Use em dashes
- Use binary framing ("It's not X, it's Y")
- Use bullet lists with colons after each item
- Add toxic positivity or "manifest it" language

**Do:**
- Keep my voice conversational and direct
- Acknowledge that spending patterns serve a protective function
- Frame everything as pattern recognition, not willpower
- Stay anti-perfectionism, shame-free
- Tighten and polish my rambling into usable copy
- Flag if something I say doesn't sound like me

**The artifact should be organized by section/page as we go:**
- Section 1: Before You Begin (Pages 1-3)
- Section 2: The Chasing Series Lens (Pages 4-6)
- Section 3: Design Your 30-Day Container (Pages 7-9)
- Section 4: The 30-Day Ritual (Weekly overviews + daily pages)
- Section 5: Integration & Your Next Cycle (Pages 42-45)

**Start with Section 1, Page 1: Welcome to Your 30-Day Reset.**

The first question from my scaffold is: "What is this 30-day no-spend challenge really about (beyond saving money)?"

I'm uploading my full blueprint for reference. Use it to know what questions come next and what the structure should be, but feed them to me one at a time.
