## Usage Decision

- The "regular" singles version (without the word "singles") will be used as the default in the journal
- The irreverent/household-specific versions go in the back for users to choose from
- In Q2 (approx. 3 months later), separate versions can be launched (couples version, family version, etc.)
- The "Essential?" column was reframed as "Need / Gray Area / Want" throughout

## Bonus Column Ideas (Not Used But Worth Noting)

**Pet Parents:**
- "Sniffed Once and Walked Away? Y/N"
- "Emergency or 'Emergency'?"
- "Would They Even Notice If I Didn't Buy This?"
- "Recommended by: Vet / Internet Stranger / Their Little Face"

**Family:**
- "Could This Have Waited Until Tomorrow? (It could not.)"
- "Forgot About It Until 9pm? Y/N"

**Couples:**
- "This Will Come Up Later: Y/N"
- "Whose Turn Was It to Be Responsible?"
