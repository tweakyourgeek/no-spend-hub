# NanoBanana Pro Background Prompts - Multiple Iterations

Background image prompts for the Simple Business Bookkeeping opt-in page, iterated through several versions based on feedback.

---

## Iteration 1: Workspace Scene (REJECTED - abstracts/workspace don't work)

Create a professional workspace scene with warm, organized energy. Muted navy and mauve color palette with sage green plant accents. Show a clean desk setup with a laptop displaying simple charts/graphs, a warm cream-colored coffee mug, and a small potted succulent. Soft natural lighting from the side. The overall vibe should feel "capable and calm" rather than "hustling chaos." Modern, minimal, with touches of warmth. Style: realistic photography with slightly muted tones, emphasizing the navy-purple (#3b3b58) and warm cream palette.

**Alternative (abstract watercolor - also rejected):**
Abstract watercolor background blending mauve, navy-purple, and sage green with cream space for the navy overlay box. Soft botanical line drawings or geometric patterns. Less literal than the Cash Envelope page, more sophisticated and business-focused.

---

## Iteration 2: Bold Floral with Mauve/Navy (WRONG DIRECTION - user wanted sage green dominant)

Create a bold, elegant floral background. Large mauve (#B375A0) and muted purple (#A5980) flowers (similar to magnolias or peonies) with warm cream (#F8F1F2) petals against a navy-purple (#3B3B58) background. Same composition style as the Cash Envelope page but with the mauve/navy/cream palette instead of sage/white. Bold, graphic florals that create visual impact. Soft sage green leaves as subtle accents. Photorealistic style with slightly artistic treatment. The flowers should be large and dramatic, creating breathing room for the navy overlay box in the center.

---

## Iteration 3: Sage Green Dominant with Full Detail (GOOD but included unnecessary explanatory text)

Create a bold, elegant floral background with a sage green base (deeper, richer sage, almost forest green for more impact). Large white and warm cream (#F8F1F2) flowers (magnolias or peonies) with mauve (#B375A0) and navy-purple (#3B3B58) accents in the petals and leaves. Same dramatic, graphic floral composition as the Cash Envelope page but with sage/deeper green as the dominant background color instead of navy. Photorealistic style with artistic treatment. The flowers should be large and bold, creating breathing room for the navy overlay box in the center.

**Color breakdown:**
- Background: Sage green to deeper forest green (dominant)
- Flowers: White/warm cream petals
- Accents: Mauve and navy-purple in shadows, leaf details, smaller florals
- Overlay box: Navy-purple (#3B3B58) as usual

---

## Iteration 4: Simplified for Edge Framing (STILL had unnecessary comparison text)

Create a bold floral background with sage green (deeper, richer tone) as the dominant color. Large white and warm cream flowers positioned around the edges to frame the center space. Simple, clean composition since the center will be covered by the navy overlay box. Mauve and navy-purple accents in leaves and shadows. Photorealistic style. The florals should be dramatic enough to create visual interest at the edges while keeping the center relatively open for the text box.

---

## Iteration 5: FINAL CLEAN VERSION (Use this one)

Create a bold floral background with deep sage green base. Large white and warm cream (#F8F1F2) flowers (magnolias or peonies) positioned around the edges to frame the center space. Mauve (#B375A0) and navy-purple (#3B3B58) accents in leaves and shadows. Photorealistic style. Dramatic florals at the edges with clean center area for overlay box.

---

## Design Context:
- This is for the Simple Business Bookkeeping opt-in page on systeme.io
- Uses the simple template with a navy box overlay on the branded background
- The center of the image will be mostly covered by the text overlay box
- Needs to differentiate from the Cash Envelope page (which uses navy dominant with sage/white flowers)
- Same bold floral style, different dominant color (sage green vs navy)
