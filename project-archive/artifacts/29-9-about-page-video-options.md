# About Page Video Options (4 Options)

For the About page carousel video, you've got options:

**Option 1: Explainer Video (AI voiceover + screen recording)**
- "Here's how the challenge works" walkthrough
- Show someone logging a no-spend day in the tracker
- Show the community, the GPTs, the monthly drops
- Tools: Descript, CapCut, or even just screen record + ChatGPT voice

**Option 2: Pattern Recognition Demo**
- AI avatar explaining one Chasing Pattern (Rainbows or Ambrosia)
- "Ever buy things hoping they'll fix everything? That's Chasing Rainbows."
- Quick, punchy, makes the viewer feel seen
- Tools: HeyGen, D-ID, Synthesia

**Option 3: Community Vibe Video**
- Montage of your graphics + text overlays
- "No shame. No perfection. Just patterns."
- Background music, quick cuts, under 30 seconds
- Tools: Canva Video, CapCut

**Option 4: Testimonial-Style (AI or real)**
- "I spent $300 at Target every week without thinking..."
- Quick problem → solution format
- Can be text-on-screen or AI avatar
- Tools: HeyGen for AI, Canva for text-based

**My vote: Option 3** - easiest to make, shows your vibe, no complicated AI needed, can make it in Canva in 20 minutes.
