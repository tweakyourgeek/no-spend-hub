## Bonus Column Ideas by Household Type

### Pet Parents bonus columns:
- "Sniffed Once and Walked Away? Y/N"
- "Emergency or 'Emergency'?"
- "Would They Even Notice If I Didn't Buy This?"
- "Recommended by: Vet / Internet Stranger / Their Little Face"

### Family bonus columns:
- "Could This Have Waited Until Tomorrow? (It could not.)"
- "Forgot About It Until 9pm? Y/N"

### Couples bonus columns:
- "This Will Come Up Later: Y/N"
- "Whose Turn Was It to Be Responsible?"

## "Essential" Column Replacement Options

Instead of the standard Y/N essential column:

- Survival / Sanity / Shiny Thing
- Alive / Functioning / Aspirational
- Need / Cope / Want
- Non-negotiable / Gray Area / Caught in the Wild

**Decision:** "Need / Gray Area / Want" was selected for the regular versions.
