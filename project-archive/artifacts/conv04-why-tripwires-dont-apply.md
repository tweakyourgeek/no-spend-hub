## The Tripwire Logic (and why it doesn't apply to you)

**Traditional funnel bros say:**
"You need a $7-27 offer to convert cold traffic into buyers so they're warmed up for the $997 course!"

**But you're not selling a $997 course.**

You're selling:
- Community membership (recurring)
- Spreadsheet vault access (recurring)
- Live sessions (high-touch, premium)

**Your model is relationship-based, not transaction-based.**

## What You Gain by Skipping the Tripwire

**1. Simpler customer journey:**
- Free lead magnet > Nurture > Community invite
- That's it. Three steps. Not seven.

**2. Clearer positioning:**
- "I give you incredible tools for free, and if you want 500 more plus support, join the membership"
- VS "Here's a free thing, now buy this $17 thing, now consider this $47 thing, now..."

**3. Less product management:**
You already have:
- Multiple brands
- 500+ spreadsheet templates
- GPT ecosystem
- Challenge workbook
- Journal
- Affirmation cards
- Budget templates

**You don't need another SKU. You need fewer things to maintain, not more.**

**4. Better community fit:**
People who join because they got massive value for free > already aligned with your "give value first" philosophy

People who join because they bought a $17 thing > transactional mindset, might churn faster

## What Your Funnel Actually Needs

**Free > Premium**

That's it.

**Free Tier:**
- Time Value Calculator (+ any other lead magnets)
- No-Spend Challenge workbook
- Skool community access (free tier)
- Nurture sequence that shows the value

**Premium Tier ($27-47/month or whatever you land on):**
- Full spreadsheet vault
- Premium Skool access
- Group sessions
- New templates monthly

**The nurture sequence IS your tripwire.**

Instead of "buy this $17 thing," it's "here are 5 emails showing you how valuable this ONE free tool is, imagine having 500 of them plus support."

## The Math

**With tripwire:**
- 100 people download free thing
- 10 buy $17 tripwire = $170
- 3 of those join $37/month = $111/month

**Without tripwire:**
- 100 people download free thing
- 8 join $37/month directly = $296/month
- Less complexity, less refunds, less customer service

You might convert BETTER without the tripwire because there's no "well I already bought the $17 thing, I'll just use that" excuse.

## Permission Slip

You have permission to:
- Give away incredible value for free
- Not monetize every single step
- Build trust before asking for money
- Let the free stuff be SO GOOD that people WANT to pay you
- Skip the tripwire entirely

Your model is: **"Sample the ecosystem, fall in love, join for more."**

Not: **"Buy this, then this, then this, now you're committed via sunk cost fallacy."**

## What to Do Instead

**Make the free calculator SO valuable that people think:**
"Holy shit, if this is free, what's in the paid membership?"

Then the nurture sequence answers that question over 10 days.

Email 4 is your pitch. That's it. One pitch. After 10 days of value.

**If they don't convert:** They stay on your list, they still got value, they might join later when the Skool community proves itself.

**If they do convert:** They're coming in hot, already trusting you, already seeing the value.

No tripwire needed.

## Decision

Add tripwire AFTER bundle deadline, when there's energy. For now, ship the lead magnet funnel without it.
