## GPT BUNDLE STRATEGY

### UPFRONT (Include with Spreadsheet Pack):

These should be the ones that help them **immediately use the spreadsheets** and get quick wins:

**Option 1: The Essential Duo**
1. **"Subscription Audit GPT"**
   - Helps them decide Keep/Pause/Cancel for each subscription
   - Feeds directly into the Subscriptions & Autopays tab
   - IMMEDIATE value - they can audit their subscriptions right away

2. **"Freed-Up Money Allocator GPT"**
   - Helps them strategically allocate saved money
   - Feeds into the Freed-Up Money Plan tab
   - Answers "Now what?" after they've identified savings

**Option 2: The Starter Pack**
1. **"Subscription Audit GPT"** (same as above)
2. **"No-Spend Trigger Analyzer GPT"**
   - Analyzes their spending patterns from the Day Tracker
   - Helps them understand WHY they spend
   - More behavioral/coaching focused

---

### RETENTION/DRIP (Monthly or Upsell):

These could be released later to keep people engaged:

- **"Maybe Later Decision GPT"** (after 30 days when they revisit the list)
- **"Cash Runway Deep-Dive GPT"** (more advanced runway analysis)
- **"Business Expense Categorizer GPT"** (helps with ongoing tracking)
- **"Challenge Reflection GPT"** (post-challenge insights and next steps)

## FINAL DECISION: Include 1 GPT with the Bundle

**The Subscription Audit GPT** was selected as the "gateway" GPT.

### Why this specific one:
- Makes the spreadsheets **immediately actionable**
- Creates **quick wins** (people see results in the first hour)
- Shows the **power of the GPT ecosystem** without giving away the farm
- Leaves them wanting **MORE** GPTs for the other aspects
- Perfect "taste test" that leads to "I need the other 17!"

### THE FUNNEL FLOW:

**Bundle (with 1 GPT)** --> Shows them how powerful GPTs are
**Free Challenge** --> They use the spreadsheets + 1 GPT and love it
**Premium Tier** --> "Get 17 MORE GPTs to support your journey!" (templates + remaining GPTs)
**VIP Tier** --> Everything + lives for accountability

### STRATEGIC RATIONALE:

By including **just ONE GPT**, you're:
1. **Not cannibalizing premium** (17 GPTs still exclusive to paid tiers)
2. **Creating desire** ("If this ONE GPT is this helpful, imagine having all 18!")
3. **Removing friction** (they can actually USE the spreadsheets immediately)
4. **Standing out** from other spreadsheet bundles (nobody else is giving GPTs!)
5. **Demonstrating your methodology** (you're not just giving tools, you're providing guidance)

### DON'T Include Multiple GPTs in the Bundle Because:
- You already have a rollout strategy for premium
- You want to maintain the value proposition of the paid tiers
- The bundle's job is to GET THEM IN, not solve everything
- Leave them hungry for more
