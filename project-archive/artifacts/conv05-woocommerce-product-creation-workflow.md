# WooCommerce Product Creation Workflow (20-Step Template)

This is the standardized workflow used with ChatGPT for creating WooCommerce product listings. Brought over to Claude for future use.

## The 20 Steps:

1. **Upload Product Information:**
   - I am uploading a file containing product details. Please review the file and extract the necessary information to create a WooCommerce product.

2. **Generate Product Name:**
   - Based on the information from the uploaded file, suggest a catchy and descriptive product name.

3. **Create Detailed Description:**
   - Using the product details from the uploaded file, create a comprehensive product description highlighting features, benefits, use cases, and unique selling points.

4. **Short Description:**
   - Write a brief, engaging short description for the product based on the uploaded information.

5. **Key Features List:**
   - List the key features of the product in bullet points based on the uploaded details.

6. **Identify Target Audience:**
   - Describe the target audience for this product based on the uploaded information.

7. **Highlight Unique Selling Points:**
   - Highlight what makes this product unique compared to competitors based on the uploaded information.

8. **Provide Usage Instructions:**
   - Create clear and concise usage instructions for the product based on the uploaded information.

9. **Explain Product Benefits:**
   - List the main benefits of using this product based on the uploaded information.

10. **Generate Customer Testimonials:**
    - Create a few realistic customer testimonials emphasizing the positive aspects of the product based on the uploaded information.

11. **Specify Tone and Style:**
    - Describe the preferred tone and style for the product description (e.g., professional, casual, technical) based on the uploaded information.

12. **List SEO Keywords:**
    - Identify relevant SEO keywords and phrases to be included in the product description based on the uploaded information.

13. **Justify Pricing:**
    - Provide a rationale for the product's pricing, highlighting its value and quality based on the uploaded information.

14. **Create FAQs:**
    - Generate a list of potential FAQs and their answers related to the product based on the uploaded information.

15. **Detail Warranty Information:**
    - Provide any warranty or guarantee information for the product based on the uploaded information.

16. **Suggest Related Products:**
    - Based on my inventory, suggest a few related products that could be cross-sold or upsold with this product.

17. **Specify Product Categories and Tags:**
    - Based on the uploaded information, suggest appropriate product categories and tags.

18. **Generate Purchase Note:**
    - Create a standardized purchase note for the digital product download instructions. Example: 'Thank you for your purchase! Your digital product is available for download [insert download link or instructions].'

19. **Focus Keywords:**
    - Identify focus keywords for SEO based on the product information.

20. **Meta Description:**
    - Write a meta description for SEO purposes based on the product information.
