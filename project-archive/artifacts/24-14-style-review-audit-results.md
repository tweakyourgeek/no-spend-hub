# Style Review Audit Results

Review of daily prompts and weekly overviews against the style guide and codex.

## Review Criteria:
- No binary framing
- No dashes (em dashes)
- No sentences beginning with conjunctions
- Conversational, not instructional
- Patterns are lenses, not labels
- No therapy-speak
- Have a sense of humor

---

## Findings:

### Possible Binary Framing:
- Day 15: "The gap is a story, not a fact."
- Day 17: "That insufficiency is a feeling, not a truth."
- Day 25: "Not failure, not proof you can't do this, just information."

### Tone:
- No humor anywhere. It's all earnest. Given preference for "have a freaking sense of humor," this was too serious throughout.

### Borderline Therapy-Speak:
- Day 10 title: "The 'I Deserve This' Voice"
- Day 22: "Name the longing, not the purchase."

### Slightly Generic:
- Day 26: "It requires noticing, practicing, and adjusting over time."

### Clean On:
- No em dashes
- No sentences starting with conjunctions
- No willpower framing
- No PLR language
- Patterns treated as lenses

---

## Fixes Applied:

### Binary Framing Fixes:
- **Day 15:** "The gap is a story, not a fact." -> "That gap has been running the same narrative for a while. Worth questioning."
- **Day 17:** "That insufficiency is a feeling, not a truth." -> "Insufficiency is loud. Doesn't mean it's accurate."
- **Day 25:** "Not failure, not proof you can't do this, just information." -> "Slips are data. Sometimes expensive data, but data."

### Therapy-Speak Fixes:
- **Day 10 title:** "The 'I Deserve This' Voice" -> "The Reward Reflex"
- **Day 22:** "Name the longing, not the purchase." -> "The purchase is pointing at something. What's it actually pointing at?"

### Generic Fix:
- **Day 26:** "It requires noticing, practicing, and adjusting over time." -> "It takes attention and some trial and error. Mostly error, if we're being honest."
