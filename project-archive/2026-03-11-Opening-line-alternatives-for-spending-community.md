**[HOW IT'S DIFFERENT]**

"Here's what we're NOT doing:

We're not shaming you for spending. We're not making you feel guilty about past purchases. We're not telling you to 'just have more willpower.'

Spending during a challenge doesn't mean you failed. It means you have data.

"What you'll get here is pattern recognition, not judgment. A place where you can say 'I spent $47 at the store and I don't even know why' and people will help you figure out the why, not lecture you about the $47.

**[CLOSING - Next Step]**

"Join free and start with our 7-Day Spending Tracker. See if this resonates. If it does, there's more here when you're ready.
